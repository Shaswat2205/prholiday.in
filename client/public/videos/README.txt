# Video Backgrounds

The website is configured to play video backgrounds from this folder.
Due to copyright and anti-scraping protections, we could not auto-download high-quality stock footage for every section.

## Required Files
1.  **beach.mp4**: (Already downloaded) Used on the Home page.
2.  **mountain.mp4**: Used on the Destinations page.
3.  **forest.mp4**: Used on the About Us page.

## How to Add Videos
1.  Go to a free stock video site like [Pexels](https://www.pexels.com/search/videos/mountain/) or [Mixkit](https://mixkit.co/free-stock-video/mountain/).
2.  Download a video you like.
3.  Rename it to `mountain.mp4`.
4.  Copy it into this folder: `client/public/videos/`.
5.  Refresh the website.
